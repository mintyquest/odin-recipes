Retro Spaghetti

Description

The classic pasta dish enjoined by millions around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 24 ounce tomato sauce
  • 8 ounce spaghetti

Steps

  1. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
  2. Cook ground beef on skillet until brown.
  3. Add tomato sauce to meat and stir until completely mixed.
  4. Once noodles are cooked, drain and mix with the sauce and beef.
  5. Enjoy!
